ABOUT ME

I’m a junior at Barnard College studying Computer Science and Architecture with a focus on design, human-computer interaction, and user-centered thinking. My work explores how technology can support creativity and make complex ideas intuitive through thoughtful interfaces and spatial tools. I’m especially interested in designing interactive experiences that combine code, design, and an understanding of how people engage with digital and physical environments.
